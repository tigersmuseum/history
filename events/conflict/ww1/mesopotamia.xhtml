Mesopotamia campaign

This campaign starts with the Fao Landing in November 1914, and ends with the signing of Armistice of Mudros on 30 October 1918.

Battle of Basra

11th - 22nd November 1914

The battle honour is Basra.

First Action of Qurna

4th - 8th December 1914

Battle of Shaiba

12th - 14th April 1915

The battle honour is Shaiba.

Battle of Nasiriyah

27th June - 25th July 1915

Battle of Kut, 1915

28th September 1915

Also known as the First Battle of Kut or the Battle of Es Sinn.

The battle honour is Kut al Amara 1915.

Battle of Ctesiphon

22nd - 24th November 1915

British retreat from Ctesiphon to Kut al Amara

25th November - 3rd December 1915

Siege of Kut

7th December 1915 - 29th April 1916

Battle of Kut, 1917

9thJanuary - 24th February 1917

Also known as the Second Battle of Kut.

The battle honour is Kut al Amara 1917.
