Allied invasion of Italy

3 September 1943 - VE Day

Salerno

9th - 18th September 1943

Also known as Operation Avalanche.

The battle honour is Salerno.

Landing at Porto San Venere

8th September 1943

The battle honour is Landing at Porto San Venere.

Battle of Monte Cassino

17th January - 18th May 1944

Also known as the Battle for Rome.

The battle honours are: Casa Sinagogga, Cassino Railway Station, Massa Vertecchi, Monastery Hill, Cassino I, Cassino II.

Allied advance into Northern Italy

June to August 1944

The battle honour is Advance to Florence.

Trasimene Line

11th June - 2nd July 1944

The battle honour is Trasimene Line.

Gothic Line

25th August 1944 - early March 1945

The battle honours are: Monte Gridolfo, Monteguadio, Montescudo, Coriano, Rimini Line, Montilgallo, Frisoni, Capture of Forli, Capture of Ravenna, Rio Fontanaccia, Naviglio Canal, Casa Fabbri Ridge, Casa Fortis, Casale, Cosina Canal Crossing, Catarelto Ridge, Lamone Crossing, Lamone Bridgehead, Pideura, and Gothic Line.

Operation Grapeshot

6th April - 2nd May 1945

Also known as the spring 1945 offensive in Italy.
