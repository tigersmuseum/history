1st Battalion, Hampshire Regiment was assigned to 11th Brigade throughout the First World War.

14th Battalion, Hampshire Regiment was assigned to 116th Brigade from October 1915 to 22 March 1918, when the battalion disbanded.

15th Battalion, Hampshire Regiment was assigned to 122nd Brigade from October 1915 until 1917.

11th Brigade was part of 4th Division throughout the First World War.

122nd Brigade was part of 41st Division during the First World War.

116th Brigade was part of 39th Division during the First World War.

41st Division was part of X Corps in 1917.

41st Division was part of XVII Corps in 1917.

X Corps was part of the Second Army in 1917.

XVII Corps was part of the Third Army in 1917.
